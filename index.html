Constitution of van

Required to be followed


  1. article 1 Don't hurt


    • sec 1:No one can beat or scold anyone except the councillor, the opposition ruler and driver.(one can after councillor's permission)

    • sec 2:Allowed people(article 1 sec 1) can only beat when emergency, unnessarily beating not allowed

    • sec 3:Bulling or discrimination can be punishable


  2. article 2 Powers of councillor


    • sec 1:Can beat anyone(in emergency)

    • sec 2:Can scold anyone

    • sec 3:Can order anyone(not for his convinience)

    • sec 4:Has the main connection with van driver

    • sec 5:Has main control on everyone

    • sec 6:Can change seat of anyone(including himself) anytime for any duration (forever also)

    • sec 7:Has to sit in middle seat compartment

    • sec 8:Can block a bully or anyone doing discrimination or bad thing


  3. article 3 Powers of oppsition ruler


    • sec 1:Can't beat(only in emergency), only scold or make understand

    • sec 2:Has to help councillor

    • sec 3:Obey rules of councillor to help him (not for his convienence)

    • sec 4:Has to solve small disputes in behind seating compartment

    • sec 5:Take place when councillor is on leave

    • sec 6:While on councillor's post, can use all powers of councillor

    • sec 7:Has main connection with councillor

    • sec 8:Can help to solve disputes in middle seating compartment


  4. article 4 Condition for being in politics


    • sec 1:Should be in 6th, 7th or 8th

    • sec 2:Should be knowing all rules

    • sec 3:To be a councillor, able one should have majority in voting


  5. article 5 Demerits of councillor


    • sec 1:Not as powerful as driver

    • sec 2:Cannot do anything to opposition ruler(except in emergency)

    • sec 3:Has to obey driver(not for driver's convinience)


  6. article 6 Punishments


    • sec 1:Elder brothers/sisters have all control over their younger brothers/sisters. article 1 cannot stop them for taking action on their siblings(except for severe fight).

    • sec 2:One can get scolded by councillor or opposition ruler when mistake is caught.

    • sec 3:One can be beaten by councillor or opposition ruler only in emergency

    • sec 4:Article 6 sec 1 is also applied on youger siblings


  7. article 7 Changes in constitution


    • sec 1:Any section of any article can only be changed when there is majority of 9 or more students

    • sec 2:Any whole article can only have change when majority of all the students (grade 2nd +) agree with the change

    • sec 3:To make change the constitution a photo of consent paper (made by councillor) should be sent to creator of constitution (Vidit Keshari).
      consent should include the name of current councillor and name of students agreeing with councillor and those students signature


These are to be followed if not then there might be action on the student (mostly soft teaching or scolding).

These rules are for well being and protection of every student in the van
